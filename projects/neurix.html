Neurix

Download

Neurix is an offline notes-taking software for Windows 10 and 11. It lets you write, save, and manage your notes directly on your computer without needing an internet connection.

All notes are stored locally, so your data stays private and under your control. Neurix is built to be fast, simple, and easy to use, making it ideal for everyday note-taking, ideas, reminders, and personal work.

There are no accounts, no cloud syncing, and no distractions, just a clean and reliable notes app that works anytime, even offline.
