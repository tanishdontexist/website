Aura Bot is a Discord bot designed around a community-driven Aura system that tracks user interactions, consistency, and risk-based actions. It allows members to gain or lose Aura points through replies, daily rewards, streaks, and controlled community interactions.

All Aura data is stored in a centralized MySQL database, enabling features like cooldown enforcement, streak tracking, leaderboards, levels, ranks, and visual rank cards. The bot includes systems such as daily rewards, risk-based gambling, Aura shields, role assignment for top users, and detailed statistics, all managed through slash commands and message-based interactions.

Aura Bot is built to be structured, balanced, and engaging, focusing on long-term participation rather than spam-based scoring. It serves as a self-contained reputation and progression system that adds depth, persistence, and competitive dynamics to H Λ N G O U T ™
