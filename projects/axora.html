Visit Axora

Axora is a secure API-based credential management service for apps and websites. It allows developers and project owners to store, manage, and control passwords and sensitive credentials through a centralized system.

All credentials are managed by the owner, giving full control over when a credential can be used, restricted, rotated, or disabled. Axora is built to be secure, reliable, and flexible, making it ideal for managing authentication keys, service credentials, and access across multiple projects.

There are no hardcoded secrets, no uncontrolled access, and no scattered credentials, just a controlled and secure password management layer that keeps your applications and projects protected.
